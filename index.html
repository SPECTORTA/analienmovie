Our project is a 1 - 2 minute mixed media film parodying science fiction alien movies of the 60s - 80s. This film utilises animation and 3D computer rendered environments mixed together to create a unique and stylized visual language. Our project uses visual, audio, and musical tropes of the era it's parodying to further the comedic aspect, increasing its appeal to a wide audience.
1
Narratively, The basic premise/story for this project is two extraterrestrial creatures managed to escape a high-security containment facility, and seek refuge within a gas station convenience store situated in the middle of a Southern desert. It is implied that the containment facility they escaped from is similar to Area 51. 2
